Hi, I'm Gabor

I'm a math phd student at UniPv, this is my personal website I use to share whatever i'm interested in at the moment, may that be math, events or diy projects.

Right now one of my main interests is Climate Change. From this stems my interest in Solarpunk, beyond growth and practices to relearn how to live in as a sustainable society. I am interested in mathematics applications to related problems such as power grid optimization; organizing solarpunky events, such as creating math activities with captivating stories connecting the students to mathematics and nature.

An other interest of mine: projective metrics theory, a general class of metrics which encompasses many metrics used in Cryptography and Coding theory such as Hamming Metric, Rank Metric and others.
